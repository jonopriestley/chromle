How to Play

The aim of the game is to find the mystery colour.

Use the colour wheel or box to pick a colour.

Click the 'Make Guess' button to make a guess.

The higher the score, the better the guess.

Click the best guess box to jump back to it.

Easy mode only needs 98+ to win.
